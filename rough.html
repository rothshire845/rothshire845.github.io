Rothshire

Founded by people passionate about providing multi-disciplinary services, our collaborative and open minded approach to design puts our clients at the heart of everything we do.

Rothshire have grown into a company that employs experts across a wide range of traditional and emerging fields. The result is a highly qualified skilled team who have the expertise for creating and developing viable and sustinable solutions who prides themselves on integrity in all our dealings with a constantly forward thinking, innovative approach. Rothshire ensure a leading service that redefines boundaries and sets new standards by producing integrated and relevant engineering solutions for a variety of clients in both the public ad private sector with our practical and cost-effective engineering solutions.

We aim to satisfy multiple and complex needs with our approach to problem solving through data information knowledge insight which fosters a relentless approach to design. The capabilities of our project teams are cultivated through a continual definition and focus on the essence, at any location, and for the project duration. Ultimately, our work ethic assumes accountability beyond discipline which far exceeds expectations which is why we never walk away from a challenge. This philosophy runs throughout the whole business and is a core value of our company.
